Latest Projects

London Wall Tunnel

Vevo has been building the over three-kilometer-long shell structure of London Wall tunnel since 2010 as a part of joint venture. Construction work was primarily focused on tow parallel single truck main tunnel. The main tunnels are being driven at staggered intervals and was flitted out with single-leaf lining segments. The construction process allowed use of two tunnel boring machines (TBM)

Causeway Bridge

We delivered award-winning Causeway Bridge using segmental concrete construction. We performed all major structure aspects including excavation, cofferdams, cast-in-place and precast concrete, beam erection, sear stud welding and slip form parapets. We used offsite prefabrication of structural elements to increase quality, save time and reduce risk. The new bridge was completed as planned
